Communication: enabling

People with addictions effect the whole family by using one of four methods.

Table 1. Sample Statements and Emotional Responses
Emotion Addict's Statements Tool
Guilt "If only you had/hadn't done _______. It's your fault I ______." Accusation
Hope "I promise to quit ______ when I get my job promotion." Bargaining
Fear/Anger "If you don't let me go to that party, I'll run away." Threat
